About the Event

Join us at the Sankranthi Celebrations! 2025 for a day to blend traditional values with modern expressions, creating an enjoyable and educational experience for students while embracing the festive spirit!

Events

  • Cultural Performances: Showcase folk dances (like Kolattam or Garba), songs, and skits highlighting Sankranthi's cultural roots.
  • Cooking Competition: Host a traditional cooking contest featuring Sankranthi delicacies like Pongal, laddoos, and tilgul.
  • Ethnic Wear Fashion Show: Conduct a fashion show highlighting traditional attires from different regions
  • Traditional Games: Include games like Kabaddi, Tug of War, and Kho-Kho to revive the spirit of rural traditions.
